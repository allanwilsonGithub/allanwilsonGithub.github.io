GAME RULES:
            
            - The game has 2 players, playing in turns

            - For each turn, you roll the dice as many times you wish
                Each result gets added your current score
            
            - If you roll a 1, all your current score is lost and the next player takes a turn
            
            - Beware! If you roll two 6s in a row you lose all your score!
            
            - When you choose 'Hold', your current score is saved to your total score, and the turn passes to the next player.
            
            - The first player to reach 100 wins the game!
            
            - You can 'toggle' the dice set
            
            - For longer or shorter games you can change the number limit.
