GAME RULES:
            
            - The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
            - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes.
                  Each result gets added to his ROUND score
            - BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets lost.
                  After that, it's the next player's turn
            - The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his
                  ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn
            - The first player to reach 100 points (or whatever you set for the limit)
                  on GLOBAL score wins the game
